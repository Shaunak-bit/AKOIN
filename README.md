# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Design Assignment

This project is a **responsive homepage and brand identity concept** for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**. The goal of this assignment was to design a clean technology-focused brand and build a modern single-page website explaining IDR’s mission, services, and community.



# 📖 Project Overview

The **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)** is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk.  

The platform aims to bridge **academic research and industry practice** by training professionals who can work in high-consequence sectors such as financial services, cybersecurity, and critical infrastructure.

This project presents a **clean, modern homepage design** that introduces the institute, its core services, and its community.

---

# 🎨 Logo Design

The logo uses a **cube-inspired geometric symbol** to represent structure, risk analysis, and resilience in digital systems.

### Color Palette
- **Orange** – Innovation, energy, and forward-thinking technology  
- **Black** – Security, strength, and professionalism  
- **White** – Clarity, simplicity, and modern design  

Two logo variants were created:
- Icon-only logo
- Logo with text **"Institute of Digital Risk"**

The design remains **clear and recognizable even at small sizes** such as favicons or mobile headers.

---

# 💻 Website Features

- Responsive single-page website
- Sticky navigation bar
- Smooth scrolling between sections
- Clean hero section with call-to-action
- Service pillar cards
- Community information section
- Contact / interest form
- Mobile-friendly layout

---

# 📑 Website Sections

### Hero Section
Introduces the mission of IDR with a headline and call-to-action.

### About IDR
Explains the institute’s focus on digital risk education and industry collaboration.

### Service Pillars
Three core areas are highlighted:

- **Academy** – training programs and bootcamps  
- **Innovation & Incubation** – research, AI governance, and future risk models  
- **Advisory Services** – helping organizations implement frameworks like **NIST** and **ISO 27001**

### Community
Describes the professionals and students served by the institute.

### Contact
A simple contact form for users to register interest or get in touch.
