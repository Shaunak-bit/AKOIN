Innovation & Risk

About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to developing the next generation of digital, cyber, and technology risk professionals. We serve regulated, high-consequence environments where expertise in digital risk management is critical to organizational resilience.

Our unique model bridges academic rigor with real-world industry practice. Through partnerships with leading UK universities and collaboration with industry practitioners, we create training programs that are both theoretically sound and immediately applicable. Our graduates don't just understand risk frameworks—they can implement them in complex, regulated environments.

Our Service Model

IDR operates through four integrated pillars that create a complete ecosystem for digital risk professionals.

Academy

Comprehensive training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals. From foundational courses to advanced certifications in digital risk, cyber security, and AI governance.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing intellectual property and future risk models. We research emerging threats, AI governance frameworks, and next-generation risk management approaches.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement risk frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, NIS2). Strategic guidance on digital risk governance and organizational resilience.
